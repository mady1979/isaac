Our Programs

Explore the rich diversity of Indian classical dance traditions

Kathak

Flagship Program

Our premier offering, Kathak is known for its intricate footwork, rapid spins, and expressive storytelling. Available in both degree curriculum and recreational paths.

  • Degree Curriculum Path
  • Recreational Classes
  • All Skill Levels
Learn More

Odissi

Ancient Temple Dance

Originating from the temples of Odisha, Odissi features graceful postures, fluid movements, and emotive expressions that bring ancient stories to life.

  • Traditional Techniques
  • Temple Dance Heritage
  • All Ages Welcome
Learn More

Bharatnatyam

South Indian Classical

One of the oldest classical dance forms, Bharatnatyam is characterized by precise movements, angular poses, and powerful expressions that convey profound emotions.

  • Classical Technique
  • Expressive Storytelling
  • Cultural Heritage
