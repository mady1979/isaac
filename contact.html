Quick Answers

What should I expect from my first class?

Your first class will be an introduction to the basics of your chosen dance form. Wear comfortable clothing you can move in. We'll guide you through fundamental positions, movements, and rhythm patterns. No prior experience is necessary!

What age groups do you teach?

We welcome students of all ages, from young children (starting around age 5) to adults and seniors. Each age group receives age-appropriate instruction tailored to their learning needs and physical capabilities.

Do I need any special equipment or clothing?

For beginners, comfortable workout clothes are fine. As you progress, you may want to invest in traditional practice attire. We provide guidance on proper attire and where to purchase it. Ankle bells (ghungroos) are provided for Kathak students during class.

How do I choose between degree and recreational tracks?

The degree track is structured and prepares students for examinations and certifications. The recreational track offers flexibility and focuses on personal enjoyment. We're happy to discuss your goals and help you choose the right path.

Can I switch between dance forms?

While each classical form has its own distinct technique, many students eventually explore multiple forms. We recommend establishing a foundation in one form first, typically 1-2 years, before adding another.

What is your cancellation and makeup policy?

We understand that schedules change. Contact us to discuss our current policies regarding class cancellations, makeups, and schedule adjustments. We strive to be flexible while maintaining quality instruction.

Ready to Begin Your Classical Dance Journey?

Join the ISAAC community and discover the transformative power of Indian classical arts. Whether you're a complete beginner or have previous experience, we have a program that's right for you.
